Bangladesh wins Test Championship match against West Indies

Bangladesh cricket team recently won a Test match against West Indies. In this match held in December 2024, Bangladesh pacer Nahid Rana took 5 wickets in the first innings to help West Indies all out for just 146 runs, giving Bangladesh an 18-run lead. In the second innings, Bangladesh put West Indies under pressure by taking a 211-run lead. Ultimately, Bangladesh won the match.

With this victory, Bangladesh is in seventh place in the third round of the Test Championship with 4 wins, 8 losses and 31.25 percent points from 12 matches.
